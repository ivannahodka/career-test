FAQ

How long does the test take?

About 10 minutes. Stage 1 — 8 questions (2-3 min), Stage 2 — 10-12 questions (3-4 min), Stage 3 — 5-8 questions (2-3 min).

Is registration required?

No. Completely free, no registration, no email, no personal data.

Can I retake the test?

Yes, anytime. "Start over" button is on every result screen.

What if I disagree with the result?

It's a recommendation, not a verdict. Check "Other options" for related professions, or retake the test.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes, fully optimized for mobile. Works in any modern browser.

What languages are available?

9 languages: Russian, English, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, Hebrew, Chinese.

Can I skip part of the test?

Yes. Use the dropdown on the main page to jump to any stage.
